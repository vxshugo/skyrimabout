About Story

The events of the game take place 200 years after the events of Oblivion in 201 AD 4. The Great War between the Empire and the Aldmeri Dominion ended 30 years ago with the humiliating Concordat of White Gold, which infringed on many rights of the inhabitants of the Empire, including the worship of the god Talos (Tiber Septim).

A group of nationalist-minded Nords, known as the "Brothers of the Storm", led by war veteran Ulfric Petrel, Jarl of Windhelm, organizes a rebellion against the power of the Empire, as a result of which a civil war begins in Skyrim. In order to make himself known to the whole of Skyrim, Ulfric kills the supreme king of the province of Tortuga with the help of the almost legendary dragon Cry, but soon gets ambushed by the imperials. No one even knows about the ancient danger that awaits them ahead.
